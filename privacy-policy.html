Privacy Policy

Effective July 30, 2025 – Simple Habit Tracker App by Blitz 25-908 GmbH

No Ads or Tracking

Simple Habit Tracker App does not display ads or utilize third-party tracking services. We respect your privacy — no personal data is collected or shared with advertisers.

Data Storage

All your habit data is stored locally on your device. You are in full control of your information and can delete it at any time via the app settings.

Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to send you reminders and updates about your habits.
  • VIBRATE: Provides haptic feedback for reminders and habit completions.

Data Safety

The app does not collect or share any personal data. It only uses permissions necessary for core functionality.

Data Deletion

If you uninstall the app, all habit data will be deleted unless you have exported a backup manually.
